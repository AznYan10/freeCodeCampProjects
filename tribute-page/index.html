He was usaully the smallest kid on the field and was diagnosed with hormone defiency. This didn't stop him from playing the sport he loved though he had to take growth-hormone injections

  • At 16 years old Messi made his first appearance for FC Barcelona, making him the youngest player to scrore for Barcelona FC. He started to become a regular starting player for Barcelona and became thier main foward leading the front
  • Messi has an all time appearance for Barcelona at 778 games and a record of 672 goals with 269 assist. At Barcelona he had won 10 La Liga titles, 4 Champions League titles, and 7 Copa del Rey titles

  • Messi has also have amazing stats with his country Argentina. He is an all time leading goal scorere of Argentina with 98 goals with 53 assist.
